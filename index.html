- Welcome to the World of Gardening! -

Here in the Great Pacific Northwest we know that locally grown is best. Sometimes deciding when and what to grow is challenging- that's why we're here!

In this website you will find a catalog of information all about the plants that like to call our soil home. This includes detailed instructions about when to plant, when to harvest, how to plan ahead, and tips on plant maintainence. We know it can be a little overwhelming form the start so we try to make it as simple as possible for you to find your green thumb.
